Average Discrepancy Per Day

Your browser does not support HTML canvas

Click to show more

This graph shows the difference between bikes checked into a station and bikes checked out of the same station. Positive values indicate that more bikes are being checked out of a station than checked into it. Inversely, Negative values indicate that more bikes are being checked out of a station than into it. Ideally, the discrepancy should be 0 so that the the bikes do not have to be rebalanced. Because the values are not zero, bikes must be balanced by moving them from negative discrepancy stations to positive discrepancy stations.
